FCBM is open to all born-again believers who feel called to ministry, missions, or deeper biblical study. There is no prior academic prerequisite — only a sincere hunger for God's Word and a willingness to grow.

Our primary intake is for the September–October session each year. Applications typically open from June. Contact our office to be added to the notification list so you don't miss the next intake.

Yes. We offer an evening and weekend programme specifically designed for working professionals and those already active in ministry. Contact us for the current schedule and schedule options.

On-campus accommodation is available but not mandatory. Many students commute from within Ibadan and neighbouring states. We can also assist with accommodation referrals if needed.

All programmes are taught in English. Some elective modules may incorporate Yoruba or other local languages where directly relevant to missions training and cross-cultural ministry.

Graduates receive a formal commissioning ceremony and are connected to our global alumni network. Many go on to plant churches, join mission organisations, or serve in marketplace and professional ministry.
