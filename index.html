Fullstature College of the Bible and Missions (FCBM)
is a premier theological institution dedicated
to the intellectual and spiritual formation of believers.
Established in 1990, we have spent over
three decades bridging the gap between deep
biblical scholarship and practical, Spirit-led ministry.
We are more than a school; we are a global training ground.
Our mandate is rooted in the conviction that every
believer is called to the "measure of the stature
of the fullness of Christ" (Ephesians 4:13).
Whether you are called to the pulpit, the marketplace,
or the mission field, FCBM provides the doctrinal
foundation and spiritual fire you need to fulfill your destiny.
